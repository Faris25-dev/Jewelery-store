About us

Mangata and Gallo was founded by Mariana, a skilled designer specializing in diamond cutting and metalsmithing. Starting as a small storefront in Austin, Texas, her passion for craftsmanship and timeless design quickly turned the brand into a growing success. Mariana oversees everything from design to marketing, supported by a team of talented artisans and a dedicated employee managing the website and social media. Together, they create stunning, handcrafted jewelry meant to be cherished for a lifetime. Explore our collection and discover the art of fine jewelry with Mangata and Gallo
