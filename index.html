Luxury Jewelry: Mangata and Gallo

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. The jewelry company primarily operates online and has a small storefront in Austin, Texas, with an atelier attached for browsing. Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look, blending timeless elegance with contemporary designs. The store offers a curated collection of rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, crafted with the finest materials and expert craftsmanship. Customers are invited to explore exclusive, one-of-a-kind pieces, each designed with a focus on personalization and artistry. The owner, a skilled designer and Austin local, draws inspiration from both modern trends and traditional techniques, ensuring every piece feels unique and special

About us

Mangata and Gallo was founded by Mariana, a skilled designer specializing in diamond cutting and metalsmithing. Starting as a small storefront in Austin, Texas, her passion for craftsmanship and timeless design quickly turned the brand into a growing success Mariana oversees everything from design to marketing, supported by a team of talented artisans and a dedicated employee managing the website and social media.
